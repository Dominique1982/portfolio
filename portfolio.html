About Me

At its core, my career reflects my passions of: compassion and care for others and a love for technology and design.


I started out progit initviding financial rehabilitation plans as a debt collector, learning how to understand people, resolve problems, and communicate effectively.


This drive to make a tangible difference led me into frontline healthcare, where I became an Intermediate Life Support Paramedic with a leading private emergency service. Along this journey, I embraced a wide variety of roles that expanded my skillset — from Regional Administrator, course lecturer, invigilator & coordinator, as well as liaison between private and government dispatch control centers.


Each of these opportunities reinforced my commitment to caring for people while sharpening my leadership, administrative, and operational skills.


The desire to help others eventually intersected with my curiosity for technology and design, which led me to self-study web development and design. Today, I channel my empathy and technical skills into DevDale Studios, helping clients bring their visions to life.
